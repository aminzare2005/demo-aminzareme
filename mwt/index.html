Specialized services across the well lifecycle

Engineering and operational support for drilling, completion, intervention, and production enhancement — built for operators and drilling contractors.

  • Directional Drilling & MWD

    Trajectory control for deviated and horizontal wells with Measurement While Drilling and modern downhole systems.

    • Directional & horizontal well programs
    • Measurement While Drilling (MWD)
    • Real-time survey & trajectory control
    • Onsite drilling engineering support

  • Coiled Tubing

    Intervention and production enhancement with high-strength coiled tubing units and integrated pumping support.

    • Well cleanouts & milling
    • Acid placement & nitrogen lift
    • High-strength CT strings
    • Integrated stimulation support

  • Stimulation & Nitrogen

    Acidizing and nitrogen services designed to restore and improve production from existing wells.

    • Matrix acidizing programs
    • Nitrogen (N₂) pumping & lift
    • High-rate pumping units
    • Coiled tubing–assisted treatments

  • Wireline Logging

    Open-hole and cased-hole logging for formation evaluation, well integrity, and production diagnostics.

    • Formation evaluation logging
    • Cased-hole inspection
    • Production logging (PLT)
    • Data quality & field QC support

  • Slickline

    Mechanical intervention for plugs, gauges, and routine well maintenance with rapid mobilization crews.

    • Plug setting & retrieval
    • Gauge & tool runs
    • Fishing & mechanical intervention
    • Routine well maintenance

  • Perforation

    Completion perforating systems selected for charge performance, phasing, and safe wellsite execution.

    • HSD & deep-penetrating charges
    • Tubing-conveyed perforating (TCP)
    • Oriented & zero-phasing options
    • Completion-ready wellsite execution

HSE & Safety

Operations built around people, procedure, and integrity

In upstream oilfield services, safety is a procurement requirement. Meridian Welltech runs field work under documented HSE controls, with 2.1 million man-hours without a lost-time incident.

  • People first

    Competency-mapped crews, toolbox talks, and stop-work authority on every mobilization.

  • Procedure-driven work

    Job safety analysis and written procedures aligned to each operator’s bridging document.

  • Equipment integrity

    Inspection, pressure testing, and workshop maintenance before units leave the yard.

  • Continuous improvement

    Near-miss reporting and after-action reviews feed planning for the next well.
